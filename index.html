Straight Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free drywall estimates?

Yes — estimates are free. Call (941) 527-5924 or use the quote form below and we'll respond with a written estimate, usually within one business day.

Can you match my existing wall or ceiling texture?

Yes. Texture matching is one of our specialties — knockdown, orange peel, skip trowel and other common Florida finishes — so repairs blend in instead of standing out.

Do you handle small drywall repairs?

Yes. No job is too small — patches, cracks, doorknob holes and water-damaged drywall are a core part of what we do.

Do you repair ceilings?

Yes, we repair and refinish drywall ceilings, including texture blending so the repair disappears.

How long does a typical drywall repair take?

Most small-to-medium repairs take one to three visits because joint compound needs drying time between coats. We'll give you a realistic timeline in your estimate.

What should I send for a quote?

A few photos of the damage or the space, rough dimensions if you have them, and your city. That's usually enough for an accurate estimate.

What areas do you serve?

We serve North Port, Port Charlotte, Venice, Sarasota, Englewood and Punta Gorda, Florida.

Do you work with landlords, realtors and property managers?

Yes — alongside homeowners we regularly help landlords, realtors and property managers get walls and ceilings move-in ready between tenants and before listings.
