Our Specialty

Texture Matching That Makes Repairs Disappear

Patching drywall is one thing. Making the repair disappear is the real craft. We match common Southwest Florida wall and ceiling textures so the repaired area blends with the surrounding surface.

What is drywall texture matching? It's reproducing the exact pattern already on your wall or ceiling — knockdown, orange peel, skip trowel, popcorn, or smooth/Level 5 — over a drywall repair, so the patch disappears once it's painted instead of standing out as a flat or mismatched spot.

Knockdown

The most common Florida wall/ceiling texture — troweled-down stucco pattern with soft, flattened peaks.

Orange Peel

A fine, even sprayed texture with a subtle pebbled look, common in newer construction.

Skip Trowel

A hand-troweled pattern with irregular ridges — popular in higher-end finishes and accent walls.

Popcorn Ceiling

The older bumpy acoustic ceiling texture. We match it, patch it, or remove it entirely.

Smooth / Level 5

A flat, smooth finish with no texture — the most demanding standard, done right for a clean modern look.

Stucco Texture

Exterior stucco patterns matched so patched areas don't stand out against sun-faded surfaces.

Small Jobs Welcome & Storm/Water Repair

Holes, Cracks & Water-Damaged Drywall? We Fix That.

No job is too small — doorknob holes, settlement cracks and stained ceilings are core to what we do. Southwest Florida also throws a lot at a roof and a wall, so we repair the drywall and ceilings left behind by:

  • Plumbing Leaks
  • AC Pan Leaks
  • Roof Leaks
  • Storm Damage
  • Stained Ceilings
  • Soft Drywall
  • Re-Pipe Patches
  • Ceiling Cracks

How do you repair water-damaged drywall? Once the leak is stopped and the area is fully dry, we cut out and replace the damaged drywall, re-tape and finish the joints, match the surrounding texture, then prime and paint — restoring the wall or ceiling to its original look.

If the leak is active or you suspect mold, electrical, or structural issues, those should be addressed first by the right professional. Once the area is safe and dry, we repair and finish the drywall.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free drywall estimates?

Yes — estimates are free. Call (941) 527-5924 or use the quote form below and we'll respond with a written estimate, usually within one business day.

Can you match my existing wall or ceiling texture?

Yes. Texture matching is one of our specialties — knockdown, orange peel, skip trowel and other common Florida finishes — so repairs blend in instead of standing out.

Do you handle small drywall repairs?

Yes. No job is too small — patches, cracks, doorknob holes and water-damaged drywall are a core part of what we do.

Do you repair ceilings?

Yes, we repair and refinish drywall ceilings, including texture blending so the repair disappears.

Do you remove popcorn ceilings?

Yes — popcorn ceiling removal and re-texture (smooth, knockdown, or a matched finish) is one of our services.

How long does a typical drywall repair take?

Most small-to-medium repairs take one to three visits because joint compound needs drying time between coats. We'll give you a realistic timeline in your estimate.

What should I send for a quote?

A few photos of the damage or the space, rough dimensions if you have them, and your city. That's usually enough for an accurate estimate.

What areas do you serve?

We serve North Port, Port Charlotte, Venice, Sarasota, Englewood and Punta Gorda, covering Sarasota and Charlotte County, Florida.

Do you work with landlords, realtors and property managers?

Yes — alongside homeowners we regularly help landlords, realtors and property managers get walls and ceilings move-in ready between tenants and before listings.
