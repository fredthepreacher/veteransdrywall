Our Specialty

Texture Matching That Makes Repairs Disappear

Patching drywall is one thing. Making the repair disappear is the real craft. We match common Southwest Florida wall and ceiling textures so the repaired area blends with the surrounding surface.

Who does texture matching in North Port? Veteran Drywall LLC specializes in texture matching for North Port homes and businesses — knockdown, orange peel, skip trowel, popcorn, and smooth/Level 5 finishes. We reproduce the exact pattern already on your wall or ceiling so a drywall repair disappears instead of standing out once it's painted.

Knockdown

The most common Florida wall/ceiling texture — troweled-down stucco pattern with soft, flattened peaks.

Orange Peel

A fine, even sprayed texture with a subtle pebbled look, common in newer construction.

Skip Trowel

A hand-troweled pattern with irregular ridges — popular in higher-end finishes and accent walls.

Popcorn Ceiling

The older bumpy acoustic ceiling texture. We match it, patch it, or remove it entirely.

Smooth / Level 5

A flat, smooth finish with no texture — the most demanding standard, done right for a clean modern look.

Stucco Texture

Exterior stucco patterns matched so patched areas don't stand out against sun-faded surfaces.

Storm & Leak Repair

Water-Damaged Drywall & Ceiling Repairs

Southwest Florida throws a lot at a roof and a wall. We repair the drywall and ceilings left behind by:

  • Plumbing Leaks
  • AC Pan Leaks
  • Roof Leaks
  • Storm Damage
  • Stained Ceilings
  • Soft Drywall
  • Re-Pipe Patches
  • Ceiling Cracks

Can drywall ceiling water damage be repaired? Yes. Once a leak is stopped and the area is fully dry, we cut out and replace the damaged drywall, re-tape and finish the joints, match the surrounding texture, then prime and paint — restoring the wall or ceiling to its original look.

If the leak is active or you suspect mold, electrical, or structural issues, those should be addressed first by the right professional. Once the area is safe and dry, we repair and finish the drywall.
